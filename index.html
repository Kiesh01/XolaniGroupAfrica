ABOUT US
Xolani Construction and Architecture Group is a dynamic and innovative firm based in Kenya, committed to excellence in the construction and architectural industries
Since our inception in 2016, we have been at the forefront of delivering top-tier construction project management and architectural design solutions. With a proven track record,
we have successfully undertaken and completed a wide array of projects across key locations, including Kenyatta Road, Murram,Thika, and Meru.
Our portfolio reflects a deep commitment to quality, functionality, and aesthetics, ensuring that each project meets the unique needs and aspirations of our clients.



Our core services include:

Construction Project Management: We oversee every aspect of the construction process,
ensuring timely delivery, adherence to budgets, and the highest standards of quality.

Architectural Design: We specialize in creating innovative and practical architectural plans
tailored to meet the distinct requirements of each project



At Xolani, we pride ourselves on our dedication to professionalism, attention to detail, and a client-centric approach.
Our team of experienced architects, engineers, and project managers work collaboratively to transform visions
into reality, building spaces that inspire and endure.

As we continue to grow, our mission remains steadfast: to shape Kenya’s landscape through
cutting-edge construction and architectural excellence, while fostering sustainable and impactful development.
